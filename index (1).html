Why I built this
I'm Swavna — a people-focused data person who believes the right opportunity changes everything.
I built JobPing because job hunting is exhausting enough without having to manually refresh LinkedIn every hour. This tool checks for new analyst, PM, and business roles across my target cities every 15 minutes and sends a clean email the moment something new appears. Built with Python, GitHub Actions, and a lot of curiosity.
How it works
01
You pick your roles & cities
Choose from the presets or add your own keywords. Select as many locations as you want.
02
We watch LinkedIn for you
Every 15 minutes, the system scans LinkedIn RSS feeds across all your selected cities and roles.
03
You get an instant email
Only when something new appears — no repeats, no noise. Just fresh listings with direct links.
